Hey there! I'm Damyan Svetiev, a dedicated Personal Trainer at Plus Fitness Centre. My fitness journey began in 2020, where my passion for helping others achieve their body transformation goals ignited a spark that would change my life forever. Witnessing the incredible impact of guiding clients towards their fitness aspirations inspired me to turn my enthusiasm in to a fulfilling career.

As your trainer, I'll work closely with you to develop a personalised programme that aligns with your aspirations, whether that's weight loss, muscle gain, improved fitness, or enhanced wellbeing. I believe in celebrating every milestone along the way, no matter how small, because every step forward is progress worth acknowledging.

I deliver tailored, evidence based training sessions and expert advice customized to your goals and lifestyle. My mission is to transform your life, empower you to smash your goals, build unshakable confidence, and unlock a vibrant, balanced life full of longevity and mental clarity.
